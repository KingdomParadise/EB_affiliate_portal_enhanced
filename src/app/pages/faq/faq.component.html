How it works

What is an affiliate program?

An Affiliate Program allows a third party (an affiliate) to advertise a good or service to the public and receive a percentage of every sale they make as commission.

What is unique about the BrandAffiliate platform?

As an affiliate you will be provided with a variety of links, content, and custom URL codes that can be copied and pasted on to your website, email, social media or pay per click account. When a user clicks on your link and makes a purchase, you will receive a payment.

Can I still join your Affiliate Program if I don't have a website?

Of course! There are many different ways you can advertise through BrandAffiliate:
1. Social Media: You can use any social media platforms, such as blogs, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, TikTok or LinkedIn to advertise your affiliate link.
2. Emails: If you have email addresses for people who may be interested in our brands, you can send them an email describing the brand’s products and services.
If you don't have a website and want to create one, check out Blogger, WordPress, or GoDaddy. *Note- We ask that you do not impersonate BrandAffiliate or any of its partners, bid on any BrandAffiliate branded keywords or use the trademarked name as your own.

Payment

How can I receive my payment?

Payments can be accessed by the affiliate under the “Wallet” tab. Fill out your banking details to link your bank account for withdraws. Once you reach the $25 withdraw minimum you will be able to begin withdrawing.

What is the payment structure?

Each of BrandAffiliate’s partners have their own unique payment structures. For details on your brand’s payment structure please visit the campaign details on the affiliate portal.

Activity

How are my sales tracked?

As an affiliate, you have a unique code that connects to each of your links. Through this code BrandAffiliate can track your sales and traffic. You can view this data anytime by accessing the "Outreach" tab in your account.

 What are the best ways to advertise a brand’s products?

The best way to advertise a brand’s products is by creating social posts targeted toward potential buyers in your friend list or following. For further information on best practices, please watch our tutorial videos in the training section.
